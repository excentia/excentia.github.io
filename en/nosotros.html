About Us

We are an IT company specialized in DevOps culture, software quality, and technology governance in organizations that are adopting artificial intelligence.

We help teams to manage, control, and ensure the quality of code generated by AI as well as by humans, ensuring that the speed of AI does not compromise system security, traceability, or reliability. We are an Atlassian Gold Solution Partner and Official Sonar Partner in Spain, which positions us as specialists in the leading ecosystem of tools for development, collaboration, and code quality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help organizations maintain control, quality, and efficiency in a context where AI is already part of software creation and automation processes.

We invest in building our own apps to extend and integrate Atlassian and Sonar tools, backed by our status as an Atlassian Silver Marketplace Vendor.

We also develop SonarQube extensions that enhance its analysis capabilities by adding new rules, languages, and reports adapted to modern development environments and AI-driven systems.
