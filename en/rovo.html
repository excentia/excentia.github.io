What is Rovo?


Rovo is a product designed with Atlassian Intelligence technology that allows you to find, learn and act on information recorded in Atlassian products and third parties, quickly and efficiently. With Rovo, users will be able to filter a large volume of business data, get concise answers and valuable information, and even help make decisions. All integrated with existing workflows and via Rovo Search, Rovo Chat and Rov.o Agents.

Features of Rovo


Rovo helps you find information quickly and efficiently and does so through three functionalities. We tell you below:

Rovo Search


Rovo Search is a tool that allows searching on Atlassian and third-party products to quickly find information.

Rovo Chat


Rovo Chat lets you participate in interactive conversations to ask questions, generate new ideas, get helpful feedback and solve problems while you work.

Rovo Agents


With Rovo Agents, users can optimize repetitive tasks, speed up multi-step processes and solve complicated problems to work more efficiently.

Find the information instantly

Rovo looks at Atlassian Cloud, Data Center (*) solutions from 2025) and connected third-party SaaS applications to find what you need quickly. Here we expand its functionalities:

Contextual definitions

Rovo’s contextual definitions are cards that help you understand unknown terms and acronyms quickly and without leaving your location. When you come across a term, abbreviation or acronym that you have never heard of, Rovo can give you an exact definition. Moreover, you will see these AI-driven definitions in the context that the term is found in. These cards may appear in the results of search or in the context of the highlighted terms.

Knowledge cards

Knowledge cards are compact, interactive information panels designed to provide information and quick connections related to searches. They allow you to delve into related topics, ask follow-up questions and discover new connections and knowledge. These cards appear as rectangles over the results in Search and offers instant, relevant information. To use a knowledge card, simply click on it to explore related topics, follow-up questions and additional knowledge.

Markers

Bookmarks allow administrators to set URLs to specific keywords. This way, the most important content always appears at the top of search results so you can work faster. Administrators can add bookmarks for common search terms and popular links. These URLs do not necessarily have to be from the Atlassian, it can be anyone.

Atlassian Rovo for GitHub Copilot

With this functionality, developers will be able to search all their Jira or Confluence data directly in Copilot or their IDE. Also allows instant code suggestions adapted to the design rules of the teams, Jira tasks, etc. All without leaving the IDE.
