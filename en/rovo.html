What is Atlassian Rovo?

Atlassian Rovo is Atlassian’s artificial intelligence platform designed to help you search, understand, and act on your organization’s information. It works as an AI assistant and copilot integrated into Atlassian Cloud, capable of connecting to tools like Jira, Confluence, and external applications to deliver useful, context-aware answers based on your business.

Unlike other AI assistants, Rovo doesn’t operate in isolation: it understands your data, your projects, and your processes, enabling faster and more accurate decision-making.



The 3 key capabilities of Rovo


Rovo helps you find information quickly and efficiently through three core capabilities. Here’s a closer look:

Rovo Search


Centralizes search across Jira, Confluence, and external tools. Access your company’s knowledge in seconds.

Rovo Chat


Your contextual AI assistant. It helps you generate ideas, solve problems, analyze information, and make decisions.

Rovo Agents


Automate complex tasks with intelligent agents that work alongside you.
