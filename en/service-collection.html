What is the Service Collection?


Atlassian’s Service Collection is a comprehensive service management solution that combines advanced tools and artificial intelligence to deliver agile, efficient, and scalable support. Designed to break down silos, it connects IT, development, operations, and business teams in a single platform, enabling the management of both internal services and customer support from one unified environment.
