What is Strategy Collection?


Strategy Collection is a comprehensive solution that enables leaders to connect strategic vision with day-to-day execution. This collection combines Atlassian’s Focus, Talent, and Jira Align apps in a single workspace, providing full visibility into strategy, aligning objectives with execution, and optimizing decision-making through AI. It allows prioritizing strategic initiatives, allocating the right resources, tracking progress in real time, and ensuring all teams work in sync toward organizational goals.



Enterprise-scale strategic planning: connect objectives, projects, and outcomes in a single central hub.

With Talent, assign the right resources to key strategies and plan your team’s growth.

With Jira Align, you can connect daily execution with strategic objectives, aligning the entire organization.
