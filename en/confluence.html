What is Confluence?


Confluence is the solution that allows you to create and share, in real time, content of interest to members of a company, as manuals, standards or any other documentation. This tool designed by Atlassian allows you to generate pages with structured information that includes text, images, videos, macros, links or attachments, among other items.

Confluence, the only source of information for your company

Encourages content creation through the intuitive interface of Confluence

Pages

Confluence pages are the space where you can write notes, plan projects and register your ideas. It is a blank folio where you will add the information and share it with your team. Pages include spaces for adding comments and you can set up tag notifications to stay informed.

Blackboards

Confluence whiteboards are the workspace where ideas get started. Like a traditional whiteboard you can collaborate, draw and visualize the information. It is a blank canvas, a space in which you can refer to other tools like Google Docs, YouTube or Figma.

Databases

On the Confluence pages you will be able to create and save databases. You can also add and consult information in real time that is found in Jira or Other third-party tools. Adapt the information to your desired view thanks to tables, cards and boards.

Loom

Record videos and your screen easily to transmit information in less time thanks to Loom. Shares updates, offers feedback and delivers information quickly thanks to its integration with Confluence. You can add these videos to both pages and Confluence boards.
