What is Jira?


Jira is the result of the recent merger between Jira Software and Jira Work Management by Atlassian. Jira is a software for managing agile projects. This solution allows planning, monitoring and managing projects and their tasks. With Jira teams have centralized information of their projects, they can easily navigate it, understand the context of each situation and facilitates the connection of the teams under a same business strategy.

Jira features


With the benefits of Jira you can align all your teams and resources and ensure that your projects are successful. Find out all its functionalities here:

Plan


Aligns teams and resources under a common strategy. Know at all times the tasks performed, the execution times, the progress of each one and the milestones of the projects.

Supervise


End-to-end visibility of all management projects. It doesn’t matter if your team works with scrum, kanban... the visibility of information is complete.

Collaboration


Jira is a powerful collaboration tool where you can update information in real time, add comments, links and attachments.

Launch


Connect all your teams so they are aligned throughout the development of a new product and its launch.

Knowledge


Gather all information generated before, during and after a project to confirm that everything is moving in the desired direction and anticipate potential problems.

Connectivity


Jira can connect to more than 3,000 integrations available in the Atlassian Marketplace and with the most popular apps: Teams, Slack, Zoom, GitHub, etc.

Why Jira?

Structure information, align team goals, divide work into tasks and know who works on what:

List view

Organize tasks as if they were a spreadsheet. It facilitates the visualization of information by viewing tasks in a single list that you can sort, prioritize and update. This way, you will have all the tasks in one place and you can filter or break them down as you wish.

Calendar

With the calendar view type in Jira, you can see how tasks evolve over time. This type of view helps you to plan and visualize the work, understand deadlines and to take a quick look at the coming weeks and months.

Sprints and backlog

Thanks to this functionality, teams can work with agile methodologies. Sprints are fixed time periods in which jobs registered in the backlog must be completed.

Timeline

The timeline is a view that allows you to visualize planned tasks, assign them within a single team or project and track their progress. This view is available for all Jira plans, both team-managed and company-managed projects.
