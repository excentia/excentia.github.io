What is Jira Service Management?


Jira Service Management is the cloud platform developed by Atlassian to bring together development, operations, IT and sales teams to respond to business changes and deliver excellent service experiences faster. This solution uses the guidelines of the ITSM methodology that facilitate best practices for managing requests and incidents, planning, assigning, monitoring and managing project-related tasks.
