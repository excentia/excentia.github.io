Current selection processes

(Last updated: March 2025)



ATLASSIAN ADMINISTRATOR/A



Description

We are looking for a person in our Atlassian team to support the department. You will be responsible for advising, implementing, configuring and optimizing Atlassian ecosystem solutions (such as Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket, among others) to meet specific customer needs. This role involves analyzing customer requirements, designing customized solutions, leading implementation projects and providing training and technical support to ensure effective use of the tools.



You will also ensure alignment with best practices and maximize the value of Atlassian solutions in customer processes.

What do we offer?

• Atlassian and SonarSource world expertise.
• Atlassian certifications.
• Knowledge of the IT sector.
• Interest in technology and digital transformation.

What do we offer?

• Indefinite contract.
• Full day.
• Flexible hours.
• Intensive day on Fridays, in July and August.
• 22 working days of vacation + your free birthday (if it’s a working day).
• Free Christmas and New Year’s Eve.
• Internal and external training.

If you are interested in this position, please send your CV to rrhh@excentia.es.

ATLASSIAN CONSULTANT/A



Description

We are looking for someone with experience as an Atlassian Consultant/a to join our team and help others do things better (or at least try).

What profile are we looking for?

• Demonstrated experience with Atlassian solutions or other process management tools.
• If you have an Atlassian certification, great.
• Experience in pre-sales.
• Experience in agile environments.



What do we offer?

• Flexible hours.
• Working remotely and/or from the offices in Valencia.
• You will be able to obtain the ACP (Atlassian Certified Professional) and Jenkins Certified Engineer (CJE) certifications. Excentia will pay for your certification and exam.
• 22 working days of vacation + your free birthday (if it’s a working day).

If you are interested in this position, please send your CV to rrhh@excentia.es.


DEVOPS ENGINEER



Description

We are looking for a person who has expertise in tool management, integration implementation, software project review and audit and DevOps process and practice improvement.

What profile are we looking for?

• 4 years of industry experience, either working in DevOps environments or code quality.
• Knowledge of dependency management with NuGet and Nexus, version control with Git and SVN, and automation tools like Maven and Jenkins.
• Good handling of code inspection tools such as SonarQube or SonarLint, and life cycle tools like those in the Atlassian Suite.

• You have implemented CI/CD pipelines and can ensure the security of your entire infrastructure.

What do we offer?

• Remote work with the option of going to the office in Valencia.
• Flexible hours.
• You will be able to obtain the ACP (Atlassian Certified Professional) and Jenkins Certified Engineer (CJE) certifications.
• Internal and external training of our partners.
• 22 working days of vacation + your free birthday (if it’s a working day).

If you are interested in this position, please send your CV to rrhh@excentia.es.
