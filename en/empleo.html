DEVOPS



Description

We know that DevOps is not a position, but a culture and a way of working. However, it is the best way to define what we are looking for. We are looking for a continuous integration and inspection tools administrator.

If you want to be part of an excellent team that constantly strives to help others do things better, this is the place for you. We are in a growth phase and need to strengthen our team. We need someone who is capable of managing tools, implementing integrations, reviewing and auditing software and process improvement projects, and above all, who is not afraid to speak their mind.

We are looking for an active person who likes to keep up to date with the world we work in. Someone with ideas and a desire to improve management and quality processes in large and small organisations.

What do we offer?

We offer you the opportunity to be part of a young, dynamic and multidisciplinary team. In addition to a stable project, a very good working environment, full-time hours, a permanent contract and a competitive salary based on experience.



Who are we looking for?

We are looking for someone with a solid knowledge base:

- Higher degree in Computer Science (or equivalent).
- SonarQube code inspection tools.
- Lifecycle tools with Atlassian (Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket, etc.).
- Knowledge of dependency management (NuGet, Nexus), version control (SVN, Git, TFS) and build and automation tools (Maven, Jenkins, etc.).
- Experience in continuous integration.

And regarding your personal qualities:

- A passion for good customer service and a philosophy of doing things right.
- A passion for getting things done!
- A spirit of continuous improvement (Kaizen philosophy: a passion for change) - Passion for what you do, enjoying your work and taking pleasure in a job well done
- Commitment and ability to make consistent decisions
- Ability to analyse technical and functional requirements.

Knowledge of the following will be valued:

- General programming skills, knowledge of Java and operating system administration (Windows, Linux).
- Knowledge of ITIL.
- Level of English.

If you are interested in this position, send your CV to rrhh@excentia.es.
