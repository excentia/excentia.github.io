Current selection processes

(Last updated: January 2024)

ATLASSIAN CONSULTANT

Description

We are looking for an experienced person as a Consultant from Atlassian to join our team and help others do things better (or at least help us try).

What profile are we looking for?

• Demonstrable experience with Atlassian solutions or other process management tools.
• If you have an Atlassian certification, great.
• Experience in the pre-sale part.
• Experience in agile environments.



What do we offer?

• Flexible hours.
• Working remotely and/or from Valencia offices.
• You can get ACP (Atlassian Certified Professional) and Jenkins Certified Engineer (CJE) certifications. Excentia will pay for your certification and your exam.
• 25 days of vacation (22 days of life + your birthday + Christmas Eve + end of year).

And many more things! Are you interested/a? Contact us and send us your resume!.

DEVOPS ENGINEER

Description

We are looking for a person who has knowledge in managing tools, implementing integrations, reviewing and auditing software projects, and improving processes and practice DevOps.

What profile are we looking for?

• 4 years of industry experience, either working in DevOps environments or in code quality.
• Knowledge of dependency management with NuGet and Nexus, version control with Git and SVN, and automation tools like Maven and Jenkins.
• Good management of code inspection tools such as SonarQube or SonarLint, and lifecycle tools such as Atlassian Suite.



• You have implemented CI/CD pipelines and can guarantee the security of the entire infrastructure.

What do we offer?

• Remote work with option to go to the Valencia office.
• Flexible hours.
• You can get ACP (Atlassian Certified Professional) and Jenkins Certified Engineer (CJE) certifications.
• Internal and external training of our partners.
• 25 days of vacation (22 days of life + your birthday + Christmas Eve + end of year).

Do you think you fit the profile? Write to us!
