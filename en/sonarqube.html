What is SonarQube?


SonarQube is an open source platform that helps developers identify problems in the quality of the code and to make significant improvements. From error and vulnerability detection to complexity assessment and full compliance with coding standards. In addition, this tool is easy to use and integrates with your existing workflows to perform checks in each stage of the product development process.
