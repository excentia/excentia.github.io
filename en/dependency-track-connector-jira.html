Software Composition Analysis (SCA) and Software Bill of Materials (SBOM)

Atlassian and OWASP Dependency Track. Better together!

Reduce Supply Chain Risk using continuous SBOM Analysis Platform along with Jira.



Dependency-Track is an intelligent Component Analysis platform that allows organizations to identify and reduce risk in the software supply chain. It takes a unique and highly beneficial approach by leveraging the capabilities of Software Bill of Materials (SBOM).

This approach provides capabilities that traditional Software Composition Analysis (SCA) solutions cannot achieve.

Monitor component usage across all versions of every application in order to proactively identify risk across an organization.

Our connector for Jira brings Dependency-Track to your Jira project:

- Identify components, vulnerabilities, risk score, CVSS metrics and exploitability.

- Create Jira issues from vulnerabilities discovered by Dependency-Track
