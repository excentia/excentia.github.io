What is Jira Work Management?


Jira Work Management is Atlassian’s solution designed for sales, marketing, finance, design or human resources teams to manage their projects and processes. No matter how complex your department is, with Jira Work Management you can easily collaborate, coordinate and deliver projects in one place. Jira Work Management is included for free in all Jira Software licenses. Plus, it includes more than 3,000 integrations like Slack,HubSpot o Google Workspace.

Features of Jira Work Management

Visual information

Visualize your project or process information quickly and easily with friendly data representation. Jira Work Management includes 30 customizable and pre-designed charts and graphs.

Easy setup

Users ensure that Jira Work Management is the most configurable and customizable tool on the market, maintaining the control you need in terms of security and privacy.

Automation

Use the automation rules of Jira Work Management to save time and facilitate the work of your colleagues so they are not lost in any step. Standardize action protocols for each process.

Templates

Choose from 23 pre-designed templates to efficiently manage projects related to marketing, human resources, finance or design.

Task management

Divide a task into smaller ones, synchronize them with projects, make massive changes and organize your work in shared projects to perform efficient project management.

Control of projects

With customizable control panels with filters and graphics and advanced roadmaps, you can track projects as easily as you can.
